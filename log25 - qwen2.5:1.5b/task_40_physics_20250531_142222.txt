\[ T = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{r^3}{GM}} \]                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                 
Where:                                                                                                                                                                           
- \( r \) is the radius of the orbit (Earth's radius + altitude)                                                                                                                 
- \( G \) is the gravitational constant                                                                                                                                          
- \( M \) is Earth's mass                                                                                                                                                        
- \( \pi \) is a mathematical constant

I need to import the 'math' module as it contains the value for π.                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                 
Code:                                                                                                                                                                            
```python                                                                                                                                                                        
import math                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                 
def calculate_orbital_period(altitude_km):                                                                                                                                       
    r = 6371 + altitude_km  # Earth's radius in kilometers                                                                                                                       
    G = 6.674 * (10**-11)  # Gravitational constant                                                                                                                              
    M = 5.98 * (10**24)     # Earth's mass in kg                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                 
    period = math.sqrt((r**3)/(G*M))                                                                                                                                             
    return period                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                 
altitude = 400  # in kilometers                                                                                                                                                  
period_seconds = calculate_orbital_period(altitude)                                                                                                                              
print(f"The orbital period at {altitude} km altitude is approximately {period_seconds:.2f} seconds.")
```                                                                                                                                                                              
