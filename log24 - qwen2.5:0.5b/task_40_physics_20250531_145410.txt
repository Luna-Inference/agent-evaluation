To calculate the orbital period of a satellite at an altitude of 400 kilometers above Earth's surface, we need to use the formula for the orbit of a satellite:                  
1/ G (T^2)                                                                                                                                                                       
Where:                                                                                                                                                                           
G = Universal Gravitational Constant                                                                                                                                             
T = Orbital Period                                                                                                                                                               
The value of G is known as the gravitational constant and can be calculated from other observational data. For simplicity, I am using the universal gravitation constant of 6.67 
× 10^-11 Nm²/kg^2.                                                                                                                                                               
To calculate the orbital period (T), we can use the formula T = 2π√(R/ρ)                                                                                                         
Where:                                                                                                                                                                           
R = Radius of Earth                                                                                                                                                              
ρ = Mass of Earth / Volume of Earth (Approximately 5.97 × 10^24 kg)                                                                                                              
Here, the values for R and ρ can be obtained from NASA's Astronautical Science Data base.                                                                                        
Let me calculate these values now.                                                                                                                                               
