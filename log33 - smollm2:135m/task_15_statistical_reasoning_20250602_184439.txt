range:                                                                                                                                                                           
z = (X - μ) / σ                                                                                                                                                                  
where X is your value and μ is the mean and σ is the standard deviation.                                                                                                         
z = (85 - 100) / 15 ≈ -23/15                                                                                                                                                     
The z-score (-23/15) means that the value is 23% below the mean. To find the area under the normal curve, you can use the formula for z-scores:                                  
z = (x - μ) / σ                                                                                                                                                                  
where x is your value and μ is the mean.                                                                                                                                         
Area = √[(Σ(xi - x̄)^2) / (n - 1)]                                                                                                                                                
Where xi are your values, x̄ is the sample mean, n is the number of values, and Σ denotes the sum.                                                                                
Substituting the values:                                                                                                                                                         
0 ≤ z ≤ 23/√(100 + 4 * 5) ≈ -23/√68 ≈ -7.98 / √68 = 10.38 (rounded to two decimal places).                                                                                       
The area between 85 and 130 is:                                                                                                                                                  
Area ≈ 0.4 + 0.4(10.38) ≈ 2.3187.                                                                                                                                                
So, approximately 2.31% of values fall within this range (between 85 and 130).
