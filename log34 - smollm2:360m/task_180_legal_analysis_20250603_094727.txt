**INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY STRATEGY FOR A TECH STEP UP**                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                                                 
1. **PRIVACY OPTIONS:** Consider registering your IP as trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets to protect your brand from unauthorized use by competitors or other third      
parties. This can include things like the logo design of your product or service.                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                 
2. **PROPRIETARY DATA:** If you have a unique business model or product features, it's crucial to safeguard this information through patents, copyrights, and trade secrets. You 
can also consider filing for utility patents on a new feature that has been developed in-house or outside of your company by others.                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                 
3. **PRICE INCREMENT:** If you're entering a market with low prices, make sure you have an IP strategy in place to protect your product from being copied at these lower costs.  
This could include things like filing for utility patents on a new feature that has been developed in-house or outside of your company by others and protecting the unique       
features as trademarks and copyrights.                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                 
4. **DISTRIBUTION:                                                                                                                                                               
* File a USPTO patent application when you enter the market to protect intellectual property from being copied at any time (you don't need to wait until you can actually sell   
the product).                                                                                                                                                                    
* File for an IP grant or utility patent if you're selling your product online, and file for a trademark registration for your business name.                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                 
5. **SECURITY:                                                                                                                                                                   
* Consider obtaining liability insurance to protect against lawsuits that may arise from using intellectual property without permission.                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                 
6. **SALE OR PROMOTION:** If you plan to sell or promote your product in the market, make sure you have a marketing strategy and trademark registration for your business name   
(you don't need a trademark registration until you are selling). This could include things like trademarks on social media platforms and advertising, as well as other ways to   
protect your brand.                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                 
7. **INTRINSIC PRODUCT:**                                                                                                                                                        
* Consider registering the IP of your product with the USPTO or equivalent regulatory bodies in countries where you will be entering the market.                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                 
8. **SECURITY OF DATA:** If you are using software for your business, ensure that it is secure and protected from unauthorized access by hackers. You could consider filing a    
utility patent on any new software features or protecting the unique code behind them as copyrights.                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                 
Remember, this is just an example of what you may need to do depending on your specific situation. Depending on the nature of your business, there are more complex and specific 
IP strategies that may be needed.
